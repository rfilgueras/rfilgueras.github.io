Where I've Been

I was born and raised in the Philippines. There I learned the value of hard work, and getting the most of my day. I started making art when I was 4 years old. I tried to draw the power-puff girls. I remember my dad saying it looked horrible then he proceeded to draw sausages that morphed into a human and IT BLEW MY MIND. From then on, I have been sketching and painting. It wasn't until my family moved to the US back in 2016 that I started to transition to digital art, and 3D modeling.

What I've Done

I decided to seize the opportunities present here in the US. The values I learned back home I applied have here. After I was hired for my part-time job, where I was awarded Employee of the Month four months after I was hired. At Harold Washington College, where I was awarded Presidential Scholar three consecutive semesters.
